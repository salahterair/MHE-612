Course Overview

This course develops learners’ understanding of planning, management, and governance within medical and health-professions education.

It introduces management theories, organizational structures, leadership models, and governance frameworks applicable to educational institutions. Participants will explore how planning and governance decisions affect quality, accountability, and performance, and will reflect on their own leadership style and management practice.

Show / Hide — Course Learning Outcomes⌄
  • Explain the fundamental theories and concepts of planning, management, and governance.
  • Analyze leadership approaches and organizational structures in HPE.
  • Apply planning/management principles to institutional challenges.
  • Evaluate governance models and accountability mechanisms.
  • Reflect on personal management and leadership style within educational contexts.

Show / Hide — Group Assignment⌄

Title: Developing a Governance and Management Improvement Framework for a Health Professions Education Institution

Select an institution and propose an evidence-based improvement plan integrating theory with practical analysis. Maximum 1,000 words. Submit via LMS within Week 3.

  1. Describe current organizational/management structure.
  2. Identify governance/management challenges.
  3. Apply management and leadership theories to propose improvements.
  4. Recommend governance frameworks and accountability mechanisms.
  5. Reflect briefly on group collaboration and leadership process.
